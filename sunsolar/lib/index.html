ABOUT US

We a Team of expertise with more than a decade of experience in Renewable energy sector especially in Land procurement facilitation, project coordination , commissioning , Engineering procurement and construction for Solar and wind power plant from medium to largescale

Our specialization covers ranging from preliminary phase for solar and wind project development Land and liaisoning consultation for Project Development, construction to operations and maintenance of solar systems. Our expertise also focuses on the technical optimization of the solar power plants.

Solar powered energy sector is developing a qualified line of international & local projects in the photovoltaic sector that are available to investors interested in the energy sector.

SERVICES

Land and project development, /liaisoning consultation for Renewable Energy Projects

We closely work with our expert team and ourvalued customers for providing best and hassle free service to our customers in terms of quality and on-time execution/commissioning of the projects

  • Identifying and Securing suitable land.
  • Ensuring safe and efficient connection of the project to the grid Commissioning.
  • Carrying out all required permitting, studies and tests.
  • Negotiating and signing a long-term binding PPA (Power Purchase Agreement) with the utility or government.
  • Project Land and liaisoning study, identification of land , site study and project consultation for project and on-time processing for current bidding proposal from the state body SCLG - Statutory clearance and legal governance permissions and approvals NOC and License and execute operations and maintenance of the project.

How we work:

Project Coordination - Complete project coordination from inception to commissioning and to operation and maintenance

Project planning feasibility study and consultation - PPFS – We offer entire study for project planning feasibility study till commissioning stage for feasibility budgeting for the entire project

Project Planning and Execution - PPE –We plan and execute renewable energy projects on our own from the beginning of procurement of Materials quality panels , inverters , transformers and structures auto tilting ( Tracker) SMB Etc executing with experience Engineers and project heads and to effective testing and commissioning to operations and maintenance
